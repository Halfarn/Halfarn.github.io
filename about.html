About Me

After a rewarding 3 years teaching English in South Korea, I decided it was time to return home. I spent a lot of time thinking about what might be the next move for me. Do I want to stay in teaching? Explore new countries? Or learn something new entirely.

That's when I decided to settle on something that has always had a great appeal to me, Software Engineering. I've always had a great interest in computers, especially being a gamer, and have always wondered exactly how everything works. That's when I stumbled upon SkillsCity.

From there, I embarked on a 12 week journey to build my foundations in Software Engineering. Throughout the course, I have obtained skills in Python, HTML, CSS, Javascript and Database technologies, and am looking forward to the opportunity to apply these concepts in a career.

Of course, 12 weeks is no where near long enough to learn any language in its entirity, but with the foundations set, I am eager to continue to develop my skills.
